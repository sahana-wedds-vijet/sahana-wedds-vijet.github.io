Vijet

Born on May 02, in a relatively quiet village Soundalga , in the state of Karnataka -India, Vijeth has been a wonderful person to get along with. He spent most of his childhood and early schooling days in Sirsi, Karnataka at his grandparents. He grew up in the rustic surroundings and this has been key to his good natured and helpful behaviour which helps him understand the joy of small things well into his adult life. After his diploma in Elec Engg followed by a bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from BVB College of Engg and Tech in Hubli, Karnataka, his hands on technical knowledge and quick wit helped him secure a technical job at Sankalp conductors, Hubli and is slowly climbing the professional ladder working with Altran technologies, Bangalore. He loves listening to music, has a good number of friends who are there for him always and has immense interest in riding bikes. He loves travelling and has completed a wonderful bike-ride to Leh-Ladakh, an adventure of a different level by itself. With his immense experience in life and technical field of troubleshooting electronic circuits, he will be there for Sahana 24x7 troubleshooting all her problems and being at her side.
